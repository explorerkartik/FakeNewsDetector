🔍 Real vs Fake

How to Spot the Difference?

Our AI checks hundreds of signals to determine authenticity. Here's what separates real from fake.

✓ REAL NEWS
✅Verified sources like NDTV, The Hindu, BBC, Reuters
✅Facts consistent across multiple credible outlets
✅Natural language without sensational claims
✅Images match the described events accurately
✅Author and publication date clearly mentioned
CREDIBILITY SCORE
High: 75 – 100
✗ FAKE NEWS
❌Unknown sources or known misinformation sites
❌Claims not verified by any credible outlet
❌Exaggerated, emotional, or clickbait language
❌Old images used out of context to mislead
❌No author, date, or verifiable source given
CREDIBILITY SCORE
Low: 0 – 40
