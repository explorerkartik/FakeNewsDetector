Know the Difference
Real vs Fake vs Deepfake
Our AI checks hundreds of signals to classify content accurately.

✅ REAL NEWS

  • ✓Verified by NDTV, BBC, Reuters, The Hindu
  • ✓Facts consistent across multiple outlets
  • ✓Natural, neutral language
  • ✓Author & date clearly mentioned
  • ✓Credibility score: 75-100

❌ FAKE NEWS

  • ✗Unknown or blacklisted sources
  • ✗Claims not verified anywhere
  • ✗Sensational, emotional clickbait
  • ✗Old images used out of context
  • ✗Credibility score: 0-40

⚠️ DEEPFAKE

  • ⚠AI-generated or face-swapped video
  • ⚠Unnatural facial movements or lighting
  • ⚠Voice doesn't match face expression
  • ⚠AI-generated images with perfect features
  • ⚠High deepfake score: 50-100%
